Materials & Technology We Support

Harness the right material for your project — from prototyping to production.

Carbon Fiber/Nylon

Carbon fiber/nylon filament is a strong composite material combining nylon with carbon fibers for enhanced strength, stiffness, and heat resistance—ideal for functional parts and high-performance prototypes.

PLA

PLA (Polylactic Acid) is a biodegradable thermoplastic made from renewable sources like corn starch, widely used in FFF 3D printing for its ease of use, low warping, and variety of colors and blends.

TPU

TPU (Thermoplastic Polyurethane) is a flexible, rubber-like filament known for its elasticity, impact resistance, and durability. It's ideal for making gaskets, phone cases, shoe soles, and other wearable or functional parts.

PETG

PETG (Polyethylene Terephthalate Glycol) is a strong, durable 3D printing filament that combines the toughness of ABS with the ease of PLA. It resists impact, moisture, and chemicals, making it ideal for functional and outdoor parts.
